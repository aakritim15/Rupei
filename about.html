About

This finance aid web app is designed to help users manage their personal finances more effectively. It allows users to input their annual salary, expenses, and other financial details, and then calculates their monthly savings.

In addition to basic financial management, the app provides insights into users' expenditures, helping them identify areas where they can potentially save money. It also features a chatbot that offers personalized advice on saving and investing wisely.

Our goal is to empower individuals to take control of their finances and make informed decisions about their money.
